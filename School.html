Since 1985

Our History

Founded in 1985 by a group of visionary educators determined to create a school where every child, regardless of background, could access quality education, [School Name] began with just 86 students and 12 teachers in three classrooms.

Today, we stand as one of the region's most respected educational institutions — home to over 2,400 students, 120 teachers, and an alumni network of more than 15,000 graduates who serve in every sector of society.

Our story is one of quiet determination, persistent improvement, and an unwavering belief that education is the greatest equalizer.

1985
School founded by Dr. Emmanuel Asante with 86 students and 12 staff members.
1998

Accreditation by the National Education Board. Enrollment surpasses 1,000.

Launch of digital portals, new library wing, and international exchange programme.

How to Apply

Admission Process

1

Complete Application

Fill in the online admission form with accurate personal and academic details.

2

Submit Documents

Upload certified copies of previous results, birth certificate, and passport photos.

3

Entrance Assessment

Shortlisted applicants sit an entrance examination in English and Mathematics.

4

Admission Offer

Successful candidates receive a formal offer letter and complete enrolment online.

What You Need

Admission Requirements

  • ✓ Completed and signed application form
  • ✓ Certified copies of most recent school reports (last 2 years)
  • ✓ Certified copy of birth certificate or passport
  • ✓ Two recent passport-sized photographs
  • ✓ Immunisation / health records
  • ✓ Non-refundable application fee of GHS 50
  • ✓ Two character references (school head and religious leader)

Age Requirements

Primary School (JHS 1)

Age 12–14 years. Must have completed Basic 6 with satisfactory results.

Senior High School (SHS 1)

Age 15–17 years. Must hold BECE certificate with aggregate 6–24 (depending on programme).

Transfer Students

All transfers are subject to available space and equivalency assessment. Contact admissions office.
